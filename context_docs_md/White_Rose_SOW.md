# White Rose Maths — Primary Schemes of Learning (v3.0)

## Introduction

Welcome to version 3.0 of the White Rose Maths primary schemes of learning! We have listened to your feedback and taken into account other national developments over the last few years to produce an even bigger and even better set of resources to support your teaching. In particular we have made progression even clearer, including more direct revisiting of previous years' work to close gaps caused by the pandemic and to align even more closely with the DfE's ready-to-progress criteria.

This document sets out the key changes to the steps in the Autumn term of our schemes. For each year group, we look at:
- Any changes to the blocks, such as order and timings
- The changes to each individual block, directly comparing the steps in version 2.0 and the steps in version 3.0

---
