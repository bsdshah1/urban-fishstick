# EYFS Calculation Policy

## Addition

> **Note:** The addition section of the EYFS Calculation Policy is presented as visual/image-based content in the original PDF, showing concrete and pictorial methods appropriate for Early Years learners. The images illustrate how young children are introduced to the concept of combining groups of objects before moving to more formal representations.

## Subtraction

> **Note:** The subtraction section is similarly image-based, showing practical and pictorial approaches for early years subtraction — including taking away objects from groups and finding the difference using physical manipulatives.

---

*This policy outlines the methods used in EYFS to introduce children to the four operations using the Concrete–Pictorial–Abstract (CPA) approach, laying the foundations for the calculation policies used in Key Stage 1 and 2.*
