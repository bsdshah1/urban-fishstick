# Brainstorm: Superpowers for Beaumont Primary Maths

Based on the school's White Rose Maths mastery curriculum documents, here are ideas for tools, features, or interventions that could supercharge maths teaching and learning.

---

## 1. Adaptive Lesson Planner
Generate differentiated lesson plans (concrete → pictorial → abstract) from a single year group and topic input. Pulls from the White Rose SOW and calculation policies automatically.

## 2. Vocabulary Wall Generator
Auto-generate a printable or digital vocabulary wall for any unit, using the Maths Vocabulary Progression document. Colour-coded by year group, with definitions and worked examples.

## 3. Times Table Tracker
A pupil-facing dashboard that maps to the Times Table Progression (EYFS–Y6). Shows which tables each pupil has mastered and what to focus on next, aligned to the national curriculum expectations.

## 4. CPA Resource Recommender
Given a topic (e.g. "column addition Year 3"), suggest concrete manipulatives, pictorial representations, and abstract exercises — drawing from the calculation policies.

## 5. Reasoning & Problem-Solving Bank
Searchable bank of reasoning and problem-solving questions tagged by year group, strand (fluency / reasoning / problem solving), and White Rose block. Teachers can filter and export for lessons.

## 6. Gap Analysis Tool
Upload assessment data and automatically identify gaps against the White Rose SOW small steps. Flags pupils who need consolidation of prior year content (aligned to post-pandemic gap-closing focus in v3.0).

## 7. Parent Explainer Generator
Produce short, jargon-free guides for parents explaining the method being taught (e.g. "how we teach bus-stop division in Year 4"), using the calculation policies as the source of truth.

## 8. Progression Snapshot Cards
One-page visual summaries of progression across EYFS–Y6 for each strand (number, geometry, statistics), useful for staff CPD and pupil transition conversations.

## 9. Calculation Policy Quick-Reference
An interactive, searchable version of the addition/subtraction and multiplication/division calculation policies. Teachers type a year group + operation and get the exact method with model images.

## 10. Lesson Observation Checklist
A digital walkthrough tool aligned to the Five Big Ideas (Coherence, Representation, Mathematical Thinking, Fluency, Variation) from NCETM. Observers tick off evidence in real time.

---

## Quick Wins (low effort, high impact)
- Printable times table focus card per year group
- Half-termly vocabulary list exports by year group
- "What comes next?" progression prompts for teachers moving between units

## Bigger Bets
- AI tutor that asks mastery-style questions (not just answers) based on the CPA approach
- Whole-school data dashboard linking assessment to the White Rose small steps
